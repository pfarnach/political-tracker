About

LobbyLibre is an open source quasi-social netowork project made by me, Pat Farnach, during my time as a student at PDX Code Guild from January to March 2015. Far from done, it was built periodically over two months, this project is meant to serve as a prototype for future projects with the intention of shining light on our elected officals, where they get their money from, and what they're up to. LobbyLibre is supposed to be a tool for the average person to directly engage with other people to discuss politicians and serve as a source of information about them that can be disseminated easily.
